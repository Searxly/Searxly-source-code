Searxly QA Build - How to Open on macOS

This .dmg was built without a paid Apple Developer ID + notarization 
(so no "clean" double-click experience yet).

Gatekeeper will warn on other Macs. This is expected and the app is safe.

How to open the app (easiest method):

1. Double-click the .dmg to mount it
2. Drag the Searxly-QA-....app into your Applications folder
3. In Finder, RIGHT-CLICK (two-finger click or Control-click) the app
4. Choose "Open" from the menu
5. In the warning dialog that appears, click "Open"

After doing this once, it should open normally in the future.

Alternative (if right-click "Open" option doesn't show):
- Open Terminal and run:
  xattr -cr "/Applications/Searxly-QA-....app"
- Then launch the app normally.

You only need to do the right-click / xattr step the very first time you run this build.
